SmartERP Local Font Setup
=========================

Place your local Runethia font files in this folder. 

Supported formats:
- Runethia.otf
- Runethia.ttf
- Runethia.woff
- Runethia.woff2

Once you copy the files here, they will automatically be loaded by the CSS @font-face declaration and rendered as the SmartERP logo font. 

If no font files are present, the system will automatically fall back to the Satisfy cursive Google Font.
